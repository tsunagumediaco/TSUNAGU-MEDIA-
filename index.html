Tsunagu Media is a video editing agency built on one belief: the best edits don't chase trends, they tell stories. While others lean on flashy transitions and fleeting effects, we focus on narrative, pacing, and emotion — edits that connect your audience to your brand.

We've worked closely with gyms, helping them turn raw footage into content that builds community and drives memberships, and we're now expanding into new spaces like cafes and lifestyle brands who want the same storytelling-first approach.
